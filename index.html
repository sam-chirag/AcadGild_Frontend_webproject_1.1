About Me

I am Shekhar Pandey, a twenty five year old designer from Bangalore City, India. I have graduated with a Bachelor degree of Computer Programming & Web Design, from the University of Madras, Chennai. Currently I am doing my PG final year M.Tech (CSE) from Amrita School of Engineering, Bangalore City. I have a passion for creating challenging, intuitive and beautiful products. My design process is very hands-on and visual.

Creating is not just a job for me, it's a passion.
